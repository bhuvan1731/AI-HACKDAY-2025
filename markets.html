Legal services in this area cover regulatory compliance, financing transactions, and restructuring. Top firms include Luthra & Luthra Law Offices and S&R Associates.

This market focuses on resolving disputes through litigation and arbitration. Prominent firms are Shardul Amarchand Mangaldas & Co. and Trilegal.

Top 5 Stock Exchanges

BSE

Established in 1875, BSE is one of the oldest stock exchanges in Asia, offering a platform for trading in equities, derivatives, and other financial instruments.

Visit BSE

NSE

Founded in 1992, NSE is the leading stock exchange in India by market capitalization, known for its electronic trading system.

Visit NSE

MSEI

Formerly known as MCX-SX, MSEI offers trading in equities, currency derivatives, and commodities.

Visit MSEI

CSE

Established in 1908, CSE is one of the oldest regional stock exchanges in India, primarily focusing on regional companies.

Visit CSE

India INX

Located in the International Financial Services Centre (IFSC) at GIFT City, Gujarat, India INX offers trading in equities, commodities, and currencies.

Visit India INX
